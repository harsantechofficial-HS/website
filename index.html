Frequently Asked Questions

What is the validity of the course?
The course access is valid for lifetime. You can revisit the materials and recordings at any time after completion.
Do I need prior UX/UI knowledge to join?
No prior experience is required. Our program is designed for complete beginners and guides you step by step from fundamentals to advanced concepts.
Will there be live sessions or recorded videos?
We offer a combination of both — live interactive sessions for real-time learning and recorded videos so you can study at your own pace.
Who can join this program?
Students, graduates, and anyone interested in starting a career in software development. No prior coding experience required!
Do I get a certificate after completion?
Yes, upon successful completion of the training and internship, you will receive a professional certificate from Harsan Technologies.
